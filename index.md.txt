# Privacy Policy – PalmOilDetection

PalmOilDetection uses your device camera to detect and analyze palm oil fruit.

## Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Camera Usage
The camera is only used when the user actively uses the detection feature. All processing is done locally on the device.

## Data Sharing
We do not upload or share any images or data to external servers.
